Understand What's Really Inside

See how processed each ingredient is with our NOVA-based classification system.

Sample Product Processing Profile
Natural Refined Processed Ultra

Minimally Processed

Whole foods with minimal processing such as fresh meat, whole grains, and fresh vegetables.

Examples: Deboned chicken, Fresh salmon, Whole brown rice

Culinary Ingredient

Extracted or refined ingredients used in cooking such as rendered fats, starches, and flours.

Examples: Chicken fat, Rice flour, Fish oil

Processed

Foods processed with salt, oil, or preservation methods such as meal, dried ingredients, and some by-products.

Examples: Chicken meal, Dried beet pulp, Meat by-products

Ultra-Processed

Industrial formulations with additives such as hydrolyzed proteins, artificial flavors, and synthetic vitamins.

Examples: Hydrolyzed protein, Artificial colors, BHA/BHT

Processing level is for informational purposes only. Many processed ingredients (like vitamin supplements) are essential for complete nutrition.
